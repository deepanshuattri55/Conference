Our Registration Service Features


Our rapidly expanding online registration service offers comprehensive features to streamline attendee registration and payment processing. Our platform enables registration managers to effortlessly create and customize forms according to their specific needs, with the flexibility to extensively test forms before going live.

  1. Key Features:
    • Creation and customization of user-friendly forms
    • Advanced pricing and fee calculations through customizable formulas
    • Ability to set different prices for various registration periods and types
    • Data import and export functionality
    • Dedicated technical and customer support
    • Assistance with registration form design

  2. Secure Payment Options:
    • Safe and secure payment methods for attendees
    • Online payments accepted in 14 different currencies
    • Wire transfers available in 8 currencies
    • Low-rate settlements offered in alternative currencies, enabling organizations to manage accounts in one currency while conducting registrations in another

  3. Permissions for Registration Managers:
    • Various permission levels for registration managers
    • Data access policies implemented to ensure confidentiality and security
    • Payment information management and decision-making policies in place

If you encounter any issues or need assistance during the process, our support team is available to help you.
