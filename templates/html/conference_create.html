How to Create a Conference


Creating a conference with EasyChair is a straightforward process that doesn't require any software downloads or complex configurations. Here's how to get started:


  1. Easy Installation: Installing EasyChair for your conference is hassle-free. You won't need to download or configure any software, or worry about backup procedures. Everything is managed seamlessly on our servers, including backup, recovery, and email notifications.

  2. Fill Out a Form: To begin, simply fill out a simple form to create your conference. This form includes basic information about your conference and helps us set up everything for you.

  3. Account Creation: If you don't already have an account, you'll need to create one. This can be done easily on the EasyChair website.

  4. Log In and Create: Once you've logged in, navigate to the "EasyChair" menu and select "Create new conference". Follow the prompts to fill out the form with your conference details.

  5. Verification Process: After you've submitted your conference details, we'll verify the information provided. This verification typically takes between a minute to 24 hours, with an average processing time of about 2 hours.

  6. Confirmation and Access: Once your request is approved, you'll receive an email notification from us. Your conference will then be listed under the "My Conferences" tab in your EasyChair account. You'll be set as the first chair (administrator) of your conference, ready to start accepting submissions.

If you encounter any issues or need assistance during the process, our support team is available to help you.
