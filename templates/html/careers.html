Careers


Join Our Team

We are excited to announce multiple job openings at our office located in the iconic 19th-century building, The Towers, situated in the picturesque Towers Business Park in Manchester. This historic location provides an inspiring backdrop for innovative work, making it an ideal place for professionals to grow and excel in their careers.

Current Openings

We are seeking talented individuals to fill the following positions:

  • Software Engineers at All Levels: Whether you are a seasoned professional or a recent graduate, we welcome engineers with a passion for coding and problem-solving. Your expertise will contribute to developing cutting-edge solutions that drive our company's success.
  • Client Engagement Specialist: We are looking for a dynamic individual who excels in building and maintaining client relationships. Your role will involve understanding client needs, ensuring their satisfaction, and helping them achieve their goals through our services.
  • Web Designer (Part-Time or Internship): If you have a flair for creativity and a keen eye for design, this role is perfect for you. As a web designer, you will work on various projects, creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites that enhance our clients' online presence.

How to Apply

If you are interested in any of these positions, please send your application via email to findajob@easychair.org. We prefer receiving CVs in PDF format. For applicants who have graduated within the last ten years, please include a copy of your university transcript. Note that applications submitted without a transcript will not be considered.

At our company, we value dedication, creativity, and a passion for excellence. We provide a supportive environment where employees can thrive and contribute to innovative projects. If you are ready to take the next step in your career and join a dynamic team in a historic and vibrant setting, we encourage you to apply.

We look forward to receiving your application and potentially welcoming you to our team!
