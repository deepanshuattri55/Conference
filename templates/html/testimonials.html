Testimonials


By 2010, I had served as program chair 20 times, participated on over 100 international conference committees, and contributed to the editorial boards of seven journals. I thought I knew everything about managing conferences and reviewing. I was wrong. Modern EasyChair incorporates vast knowledge derived from feedback across thousands of conferences and diverse communities. It is designed with its users in mind because its main designer is also one of its primary users.

- Andrei Voronkov (CEO of EasyChair, Professor at the University of Manchester and TU Wien)
